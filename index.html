About Me

Currently pursuing an MS by Research in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Bombay, my work revolves around Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP). My research is centered on Text-to-Speech (TTS) technology, exploring innovative approaches to enhance AI-driven speech synthesis. Passionate about pushing the boundaries of machine learning, I strive to contribute to the future of human-computer interaction.
